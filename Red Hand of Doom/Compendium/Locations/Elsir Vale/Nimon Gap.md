# Nimon Gap

Resides in [[Elsir Vale]]

Known for their apple orchards and sheep herders.

## Points of Interest
- Home of the [[Harvest Festival]]
- The graveyard is in the south of town.
- The Squinting Beholder Inn, owned by [[Crucia Cromwell]]

## Population
- 35% Halfling
- 65% Human
